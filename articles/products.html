X Games

Nyjah Huston has also competed in multiple X Games. To name a few, multiple X Games XIX, X Games 2014 monster energy And X games outside the US. So he is no stranger to competing, through XGames smaller jams, and SLS. Overall, he's earned three million dollars from competing. Nyjah also went to the first Olympics in Tokyo. And im sure he's going to the next Olympics too. Which will be a fun event to see.

Nike Sponsorship

Nyjah got sponsored by Nike in 2015. Since than Nyjah's has gotten his first signature shoe. The designers thought about every possible aspect when designing the shoe. The sole of the shoe is made to expand and contract when pressure is applied.

Random Facts

Nyjah Huston has so many little facts that are hard to fit under one topic. So why not make is own topic? First of all, he skated Goofy. Nyjah has his own private indoor skatepark in California. The skatepark has many assets of skateboarding that make the park great. It has rails, ramps, a smooth floor and more. Something even crazier than that though, Nyjah has skated on top of and over a Lamborghini! He also has his own skateboarding brand called Disorder. His first deck batch sold out in less than 3days. Believe it or not, he has never broke a bone. Not even once! All the times he's slammed and not even one broken bone is crazy! Nyjah's favorite trick is a Nollie Frontside Air.
