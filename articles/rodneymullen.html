Rodney Mullen

Posted by Ja'Crispy on April 29th, 2022

Its very apparent that skateboarding has come a long way from the start. One man is partly responsible for where it is today, the same man who invented many flatground tricks. Rodney Mullen has transformed street skating as it is today.

The start

Rodney Mullen started skateboarding at the age of 10, in 1977. His father wouldnt let him skateboard if he got hurt, so he had to spend his hours in the garage with protective gear working on skateboarding. Than, one year later, he placed 5th in a competition. He was noticed by the owner of a skate company called "Walker Skateboards" Which is when he got his first ever sponsor.

Tricks he Invented

Rodney Mullen isnt known as the Godfather of skating for nothing, he invented multiple tricks that are vital to street skating today. One of the most known trick outside of Skatebording, the Kickflip, was made by him. He also invented the handless 50-50 grind. He even invented the 360 flip, also known as the "Tre Flip". He invented the Backside flip, which is a 180 kickflip. But he even invented Heelflips as well. And thats only a fraction of what he has created.
